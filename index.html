Greater Shadow Protection Potions ?

Find herbs , hate kingsblood .

You'll need to make Shadow Oil .

Check out the table below to see how. To get this stuff you'll need to farm herbs in Scarlet Monastery

Shadow Oil

=

Fadeleaf x 4

Grave Moss x 4

Leaded Vial x 1

Once you've created shadow oil, you can make Greater Shadow Protection Potions

Greater Shadow Protection Potion

=

Shadow Oil x 1

Dreamfoil x 4

Crystal Vial x 1

Farming Scarlet Monastery

Run through SM in this order:

  • Graveyard
  • Armory
  • Library




Make sure you reset after doing all three by right clicking your portrait and selecting Reset all instances.
